## What It Does

Car Reels Creator lets a car salesperson or dealership team take photos from their existing vehicle listings, upload them into the app, and generate a ready-to-post 9:16 vertical video reel — complete with AI-written copy, animated slides, and a branded call-to-action.

No video editing software. No creative agency. No waiting.

## The Problem It Solves

Most car salespeople know they should be posting on TikTok and Instagram Reels. Most don't — because creating a professional-looking video requires editing tools, design skills, and time they don't have.

Car Reels Creator removes every step between "I have photos of this car" and "I have a ready-to-post reel."

## How It Works

### Step 1 — Enter your vehicle details
Fill in the year, make, model, price, mileage, and key features. Select your dealership name, salesperson label, and location.

### Step 2 — Upload your photos
Drag and drop 3–6 photos from the vehicle listing. No new photography needed — use what's already on your dealership's website.

### Step 3 — Generate AI copy
Click Generate. In seconds, the app produces:
- 5 hook options (scroll-stopping opening lines)
- 3 call-to-action options for the outro
- 10 feature callouts to display across slides
- Short, balanced, and informative caption options
- Two sets of 5 hashtags (local and niche)

### Step 4 — Customize
Select your preferred hook, CTA, and features. Edit any text directly. Choose your brand color and content style.

### Step 5 — Render and download
Click Render Reel. The app generates a 9:16 MP4 video — fully animated, branded, and ready to upload directly to TikTok or Instagram Reels. Rendering takes 1–3 minutes.

### Step 6 — Post
Upload the MP4. Copy and paste the generated caption and hashtags. Done.

## Key Features

### AI-Generated Copy
Every reel includes AI-written hooks, calls-to-action, captions, and hashtags tailored to the specific vehicle, dealership, and location. Copy is editable before rendering.

### Multiple Style Presets
Choose the content style that fits the vehicle and audience:
- **Clean Dealer** — professional, straightforward
- **Luxury** — premium, polished tone
- **Truck Tough** — bold, rugged, capability-focused
- **Family SUV** — practical, safety-forward
- **Budget Value** — deal-focused, accessible
- **Performance** — fast-paced, enthusiast-driven
- **Bilingual Lite** — mixed English/Spanish tone

Each preset controls the typography, transitions, tone of voice, and layout automatically.

### Animated Slides
Photos are displayed as animated slides with smooth transitions — zoom, pan, flash-cut, blur snap, and more. Each photo becomes a slide with the vehicle title, price, and two unique feature callouts.

### Branded Outro Slide
The final slide displays:
- Your dealership name and salesperson name
- The selected call-to-action (large, prominent)
- DM keyword badge (e.g., "DM INTERESTED") for auto-responder integrations
- CTA extra note (financing info, bonus cash, special pricing, etc.)

### Feature Rotation
Up to 6 features rotate across slides — 2 per slide — so every slide shows different information and the reel never feels repetitive.

### Text Color Customization
Choose from 22 preset brand colors or enter any hex code to match your dealership's brand.

### Caption and Hashtag Generator
Three caption lengths (Short, Balanced, Informative) and two hashtag sets (Local geo-targeted, Niche community) generated per vehicle. Copy-paste ready.

### Invite-Only Access
Access is controlled by invitation. Only approved users can log in and render videos. Render quotas can be set per user.
